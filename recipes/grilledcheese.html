Perfect grilled cheese sandwich

How can you make a simple grilled cheese better?

The best way to improve on a great grilled cheese is to sprinkle some cheese on the outside of the bread. It creates a super-crisp, cheesy crust.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 slices of bakery Pullman bread
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 1/2 pound sliced Swiss cheese, preferably Gruy√®re
  • 8 ounces thinly sliced ham
  • Dill pickle slices (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup apricot preserves

Steps

  1. Butter each slice of bread and sprinkle with Parmigiano, pressing to help it adhere.
  2. Invert the slices onto a work surface, cheese side down.
  3. Top 4 of the slices with the Swiss cheese, ham and pickles.
  4. Mix the mustard and preserves and spread on the remaining 4 slices of bread.
  5. Close the sandwiches and griddle over moderate heat, turning, until golden, crisp on the outside and melted inside, about 3 minutes.
  6. Cut in half and serve right away.
